Frequently Asked Questions

Is this study safe for my child?

Yes, all clinical studies are carefully designed with safety as the top priority. Our study is conducted under strict medical supervision and follows all regulatory guidelines to ensure participant safety.

Do we get paid for participating?

Yes, participants may receive compensation for their time and travel expenses. The exact amount varies based on study requirements and will be discussed during the screening process.

What happens during study visits?

Study visits typically include medical examinations, questionnaires about symptoms, and monitoring of the investigational medication. All procedures are explained in detail before they occur.

Can we withdraw from the study?

Absolutely. Participation is completely voluntary, and you or your child can withdraw from the study at any time for any reason without affecting your future medical care.
