Is your child living with IBS-C?

If your child is between the ages of 12 and 17 and has been diagnosed with Irritable Bowel Syndrome with Constipation (IBS-C), they may qualify for a clinical research study evaluating an investigational medication.

What's Included:

  • Study-related exams, procedures, and medications at no cost
  • Possible compensation for time and travel
